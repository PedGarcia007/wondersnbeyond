What is Magic?

Magic is a mysterious force that exists in many worlds and dimensions. It is the power to influence
events and outcomes through supernatural means. In this web app, we explore the wonders of magic
and the various aspects associated with it. You'll find information on spells, deities, potions,
and even have the opportunity to share your own magical experiences.

What to Find in this Web App

  • Spell List: Browse through a collection of spells, each with its unique purpose and required materials.
  • Deities: Learn about powerful deities associated with magic and their values.
  • Potions: Discover potions that have various purposes and learn about the materials needed to create them.
  • Spell Share: Share your own magical experiences, spells, and insights with the community.
